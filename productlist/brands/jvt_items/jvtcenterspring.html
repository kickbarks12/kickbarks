JVT CENTER SPRING FOR NMAX/AEROX
  • Enhanced Performance: The JVT Center Spring improves the torque and acceleration of your scooter by adjusting the engagement point of the clutch. Higher RPM ratings (1200 and 1500) typically provide more aggressive acceleration compared to the 1000 RPM spring​​.
  • Durability: Known for its heavy-duty construction, the JVT Center Spring is designed to withstand the stresses of high-performance riding, making it a reliable choice for both daily commuting and more demanding riding conditions​ ​.
  • Compatibility: This center spring is compatible with a variety of scooter models, including popular ones like the Yamaha NMAX, Aerox, and Honda Click/PCX/ADV. This makes it a versatile upgrade for many scooters on the market​​.
  • Installation and Usage:
    • Installation: Installing the JVT Center Spring typically requires some mechanical know-how or professional assistance to ensure it is fitted correctly and safely. Improper installation can lead to suboptimal performance or damage to the scooter​​.
    • Performance Tuning: Selecting the appropriate RPM rating is crucial. A higher RPM spring (e.g., 1500 RPM) will engage the clutch at a higher engine speed, providing a more aggressive take-off, which is ideal for sporty riding.

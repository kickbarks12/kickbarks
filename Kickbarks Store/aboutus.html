About Us

Welcome to Kickbarks Motor Shop, located in the heart of Taguig. Established in 2025, our company is committed to delivering reliable, high-quality motorcycles, parts, and maintenance services to riders and motorcycle enthusiasts across the community. At Kickbarks Motor Shop, we offer a wide selection of brand-new and pre-owned motorcycles, genuine spare parts, riding gear, and premium accessories tailored to meet the diverse needs of our customers. With a team of skilled and experienced technicians, we ensure that every motorcycle is serviced with precision and care, guaranteeing safety, durability, and optimal performance on the road. We believe that every ride is more than a journey—it is a representation of freedom, passion, and dedication. With this philosophy, we strive to maintain a professional environment built on trust, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional motorcycle products and services that promote safe, reliable, and enjoyable riding experiences. We aim to continuously enhance customer satisfaction through quality craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and outstanding customer care.

Vision

Our vision is to become one of the most trusted and respected motorcycle shops in the region, recognized for excellence, customer loyalty, and our contribution to the growth and safety of the riding community.

  • Integrity – We uphold honesty and transparency in every transaction and service we provide.

  • Customer Commitment – We prioritize strong relationships and exceptional service to ensure total customer satisfaction.
  • Innovation – We continuously improve and adapt to provide modern and effective solutions for our riders.
  • Safety – We emphasize responsible riding and reliable maintenance to protect every rider on the road.
  • Professionalism – We work with discipline, respect, and dedication in everything we do.

About Kickbarks Motoshop

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Kickbarks Motoshop started as a passion project by a group of motorcycle enthusiasts who saw the need for high-quality, reliable aftermarket parts at fair prices.

We began small — operating from a garage — but our commitment to excellent customer service, genuine products, and fast delivery quickly earned the trust of riders across the country.

Today, we carry top brands like CWORKS, JVT, Racing Boy (RCB), RS8, and more. Whether you're upgrading your scooter for daily commuting or building a performance machine, Kickbarks is here to help you ride better.
